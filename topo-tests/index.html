Topo Tests

Three approaches to the Stage background. Same cream canvas, same line color, different ideas about how the lines behave.

01 / Generative

Noise-field topography

Real cartographic contours derived from a 2D simplex-noise field. Lines morph organically over a slow cycle. One coherent map, no tiling.
02 / Interactive

Cursor-reactive displacement

Contour lines bend around the cursor like a soft force field. Calm at rest, alive as you move. Ties into the AR/wearable theme.
03 / Composed

Single composition with parallax depth

One intentionally-composed asymmetric topo, layered at multiple depths. Slow drift + mouse-tracked parallax. Looks designed, not filled.
